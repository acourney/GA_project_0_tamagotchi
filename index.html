How to Play:

Use the "Select" button to cycle through the navigation background.
When your action choice is highlighted, press "Enter".
You will be notified that your Tamagotchi's hunger, sleepiness or boredom levels have decreased.

Don't let any of these reach level 10!

You can check these levels by selecting the info panel.
